# 🌵 Cactus 🌵

Welcome to the official GitHub page of the **Cactus Team**! We are a passionate group of hobby developers who mainly work on the multi-purpose client-side mod, **Cactus Mod**.

## 🌟 Who We Are

We are a team of Minecraft enthusiasts and hobby developers who love to tinker with the game and improve it for everyone. Our goal is to create things for fun and for everyone, often related to minecraft.

## 🎮 What We Do

Our main project is the **Cactus Mod**, a versatile client-side mod designed to improve various aspects of Minecraft gameplay. The Cactus Mod boasts a variety of features to improve Minecraft:

### Features of Cactus Mod:
- **Toggleable Features (Modules)**: Customize your gameplay with easily switchable modules (often referred to as mods in other clients).
- **Custom Commands**: Use commands with a unique prefix (`#` by default) to differentiate from vanilla commands.
- **Macros**: Automate command sequences with simple configurable macros.
- **Account & Skin Changer**: Quickly switch between different accounts and skins.
- **Server Management**: Easily create and manage locally hosted servers.
- **Custom Non-Buyable Cosmetics**: Personalize your character with unique cosmetics.
- **Content Packs**: Enable or disable built-in features to suit your preferences.
- **Addons**: Expand the mod with additional features, made by users for users (Store coming soon).

Unlike many other clients, we like to keep it simple. The entire UI is designed in a classic Minecraft style without any "ultra clean" designs. You can enable or disable built-in features using our content packs feature. For example, if you don't want the "direct connect" server preview widget, you can simply disable it.
